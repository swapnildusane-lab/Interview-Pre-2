About Us

About KM Hospital

Kalptaru Multi Speciality Hospital (KM Hospital), a unit of Happy Life Medical Service LLP, is a state-of-the-art 105-bed healthcare centre established in 2025 in Sangamner, Dist. Ahmednagar, Maharashtra.

With ICU, CCU and NICU facilities, 3 modular operation theatres and 30+ specialties, we provide comprehensive healthcare under one roof — making advanced medical care accessible to every patient in the region.

Vision

To become a centre of excellence in medical health care services.

Mission

Extending affordable, advanced and appropriate healthcare to all.

Core Values

Quality, competence, trust, honesty, mutual respect and ethics.
